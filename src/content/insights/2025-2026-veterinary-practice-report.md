### **HLB Ireland’s 2025 - 2026 Veterinary Practice Report brings together the insights of over 100 veterinary professionals nationwide, showcasing** **how practices are performing, where challenges are intensifying, and what leaders are prioritising for the years ahead.**

• 65% report that a corporate buyer has already acquired a practice in their local area  
• 46% are unsure of their current practice valuation  
• 43% have no identified successor in place  
• 45% now employ more than ten staff, signalling a shift in practice structure and management

The report examines the current operating environment for veterinary practices in Ireland – covering ownership trends, valuations, workforce considerations, and long-term planning.
