### **Bruce Stanley Appointed to Lead HLB International’s Arts & Entertainment Group**

**Date: 18th March 2025:** HLB Ireland is delighted to congratulate Bruce Stanley, Partner and Head of Arts, Entertainment & Sport at HLB Ireland, on his appointment to lead HLB International’s Arts & Entertainment sector group. This appointment underscores Bruce’s expertise and HLB Ireland’s commitment to supporting the global creative industries. HLB International is a top ten global network of advisory firms working in over 150 countries worldwide, of which HLB Ireland is a member.

The HLB Arts & Entertainment group is committed to championing the interests of creatives and businesses by comparing international landscapes and best practices and identifying emerging trends, challenges, and opportunities shaping the industry's future. Through its global network, the group provides valuable insights and guidance for professionals operating across borders, connecting a community of specialists who offer expert support in taxation, finance, and business advisory.

In this role, Bruce will lead the global arts and entertainment group, which includes Ilan Haimoff (HLB USA—GHJ), Scott Bartolf (HLB USA—Withum), Ludmilla Frangu (HLB Spain—Bove Montero), and Alex Roberts (HLB Australia—Mann Judd). Together, supported by the global network, they will concentrate on key initiatives to support professionals and businesses in the film, television, music, performing arts, and other entertainment sectors.

This appointment reinforces HLB Ireland’s leadership in the sector and its commitment to supporting Ireland’s vibrant arts and entertainment industries while contributing to the global conversation on industry best practices.

For more information on HLB Ireland’s Arts & Entertainment services, visit: www.hlb.ie

**About HLB Ireland**  
HLB Ireland is a leading advisory and accounting firm that provides expert audit, tax, and consulting services to ambitious businesses. **About HLB International**  
HLB International is the **8th largest global** network of independent advisory and accounting firms, operating in 150 countries. With a collective commitment to innovation and client success, HLB firms work together to provide exceptional cross-border solutions, enabling businesses to thrive in an ever-evolving global landscape.

**About Bruce Stanley**

Bruce Stanley is a Tax Consulting Partner and Head of Arts, Entertainment & Sport at HLB Ireland, specialising in corporate structuring and personal tax planning for entrepreneurs and owner-managed businesses. With over 20 years of experience, he provides bespoke commercial tax solutions across various sectors, including Arts & Entertainment, Hotels and Leisure, Property Investment, Professional Services, Medical, and Retail. Bruce serves on the boards of the Irish National Opera and Rough Magic, one of Ireland's leading independent theatre companies. He is also a former Chairperson of Graphic Studio Dublin, the country's oldest fine art print studio.  As part of the 8th largest global accounting and advisory network, Bruce leverages HLB’s international expertise to support clients with cross-border tax solutions.
