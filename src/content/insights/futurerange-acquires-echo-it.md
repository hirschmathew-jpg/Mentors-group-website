*Originally published by [FutureRange](https://futurerange.ie/blog/futurerange-acquires-echo-it/).*

Echo I.T. has built a strong reputation for trusted service and long-standing client relationships across the region. Both organisations already share a close working relationship, particularly in cyber security, with strong alignment across platforms and service delivery.

We are delighted to welcome both the Echo IT team and clients to FutureRange.

This marks another step in our all-island growth strategy as we continue to invest in our people, capabilities and regional presence, including plans to create up to 50 new roles over the next 24 months.
