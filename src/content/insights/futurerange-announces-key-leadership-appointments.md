**Press Release**  
**For Immediate Release**

Danny McEntee brings over 15 years of senior technology leadership experience to his role as Managing Director. His dynamic leadership style and proven track record in driving business growth make him the perfect fit to lead FutureRange into its next chapter.

**Speaking about his new role, Danny McEntee said:**

_“I am honoured to take on this role at such an exciting time for FutureRange. We are committed to confidently helping our clients navigate digital transformation and cybersecurity challenges, and I look forward to leading our exceptional team as we continue to grow and innovate.”_

Michael Rooney, who previously served as Managing Director, has chosen to transition into the role of CTO. This will enable him to focus on his core expertise: driving FutureRange’s technology and innovation vision forward. In his new role, Michael will oversee the development and implementation of advanced technology strategies, ensuring the company remains at the forefront of emerging trends and consistently delivers tailored, high-value solutions to its clients.

**Michael Rooney commented:**

_“As CTO, I am excited to drive FutureRange’s technology strategy and ensure we remain at the forefront of industry advancements. Our focus will continue to be on delivering solutions that empower our clients to achieve their goals securely and efficiently.”_

**Looking to the Future**  
With these appointments, FutureRange is entering a new phase of growth and opportunity. The company is energised by the renewed focus of its leadership team and remains steadfast in its mission to deliver value, innovation, and service excellence to its clients.
