### **HLB Cyber News Insights January 2025**

In This Issue:

**1\. Increase in Cybersecurity Incidents Across Ireland**

[Read more on the Irish Examiner](https://www.irishexaminer.com/news/arid-41510612.html)

**2\.** **Government Data Breaches**

Irish government departments reported 6,885 data breaches over the past decade, with 862 from the Department of Justice. Issues included lost devices, unauthorised disclosures, and human error. Efforts are underway to improve data protection and staff training.

[Read more on RTÉ](https://www.rte.ie/news/business/2024/1030/1478044-data-breach/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

**3\. Cabot Financial Cyberattack**

A significant cyberattack on **Cabot Financial** resulted in the theft of **394,000 data files**, including sensitive personal information. This breach highlights the ongoing risks businesses face and the importance of comprehensive security strategies.

[Find out more on The Irish Times](https://www.irishtimes.com/business/2024/11/28/cyberattack-on-debt-acquisition-firm-involved-theft-of-394000-data-files-court-hears/)

**4\. New EU Cybersecurity Regulations in Force**

The **EU Cyber Resilience Act**, which came into effect on **10th December 2024**, mandates stricter cybersecurity requirements for all products with digital components. Irish businesses have a **three-year deadline** to achieve compliance.

[Learn more from the European Commission](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/cyber-resilience-act)
