HLB Ireland has completed the acquisition of Leahy O'Riordan Chartered Accountants, a Dublin practice, in a deal finalised in April 2026. The move brings HLB Ireland's headcount to 180 professionals.

## A growing group

The acquisition is the twelfth completed by HLB Ireland in the past seven years, in a programme that began with the firm's first major merger — Conlan Crotty Murray — in 2019. Six of those transactions have been completed in the fourteen months since Cardinal Capital took a minority investment in the firm in April 2025.

> "We're very much focused on building one integrated professional services business managed and controlled in Ireland."
>
> — Mark Butler, Managing Partner

Fee revenue has doubled over the past twelve months and is set to double again in the two years ahead, bringing the firm's goal of becoming one of Ireland's top ten professional services firms within reach.

## People first

Every practice that joins the group keeps what its clients value — the relationships, the team and the standards — while gaining access to the group's wider service lines, from M&A advisory and tax planning to succession planning and technology.

> "People are central to all of our integrations."
>
> — Mark Butler

## About Mentora Group

Mentora Group is the holding company of HLB Ireland, an audit, accounting and tax advisory firm, and FutureRange, an IT services firm. Formed in 2026, the group serves owner-managed businesses and private clients across Ireland.
