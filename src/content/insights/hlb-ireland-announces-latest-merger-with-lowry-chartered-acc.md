*Originally published by [HLB Ireland](https://www.hlb.ie/hlb-ireland-announces-latest-merger-with-lowry-chartered-accountants/).*

Lowry Chartered Accountants has earned a reputation for its client-centric approach and commitment to delivering professional services.

Mark Butler, Managing Partner of HLB Ireland, expressed his enthusiasm about the merger, stating, "We are delighted to unite with Lowry Chartered Accountants. This merger brings together two firms with complementary strengths and shared values, focusing on delivering exceptional client services. Like our team, Lowry firmly believes that clients should consistently have access to partners focused on guiding growth-oriented businesses".

The merger reflects the firm's dedication to growth, innovation, and delivering unparalleled client value. The combined entity aims to solidify its position as a leading accounting and advisory firm in Ireland.

Peter Lowry, founding partner of Lowry Chartered Accountants, said, "We have been committed to delivering customised accounting and advisory services to our clients. By joining forces with HLB Ireland, we can further enhance our offerings and better support the growth and success of the businesses we work with.”

David Bolger, who joins HLB Ireland as a partner, said, "HLB Ireland allows our clients to access a broader range of services, enhanced resources, and a global network of professionals."

Mark Butler added, "HLB Ireland has completed several transactions over the past five years, and we are currently in discussions with other Irish firms as part of our growth strategy." He concluded, "This latest merger underscores our commitment to innovation and exceptional client service. We remain dedicated to expanding our offerings and upholding our position as a leading accounting and advisory firm in Ireland."

##### [**About HLB Ireland:**](https://www.hlb.ie/about/)

HLB Ireland is a leading advisory & accounting firm working with ambitious domestic and overseas businesses in Ireland and specialising in several key sectors such as arts & entertainment, food & agriculture, technology, manufacturing, and private clients with traditional accounting and assurance services along with tax consultancy, outsourcing and cyber security. HLB Ireland is an independent member of HLB International, a global network ranked 8th worldwide.

##### **About Lowry Chartered Accountants:** 

Lowry Chartered Accountants, founded by Peter Lowry, is an advisory and accountancy firm catering to individual clients and growth-focused businesses across Ireland.
