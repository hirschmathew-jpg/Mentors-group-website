### HLB Ireland Proudly Backs Focus Ireland’s Sponsor a Star Campaign

](https://www.hlb.ie/graduate-programme-2024/)

Focus Ireland is a dedicated organisation that assists individuals who are homeless or at risk of homelessness throughout Ireland. Their mission extends beyond providing immediate help. They aim to address the root causes of homelessness, challenge societal perceptions, and improve how homelessness is addressed.

The Christmas tree at the top of Grafton Street becomes a beacon of hope during the holiday season. Each star represents a commitment to bring warmth and hope to families facing difficult times. Since 2001, these stars have significantly raised funds for Focus Ireland's crucial services in combating homelessness.

**If you would like to donate to Focus Ireland and make a difference to families during the holiday period, please click here: [https://joinus.focusireland.ie/event/sponsor-a-star/christmas-gift](https://joinus.focusireland.ie/event/sponsor-a-star/christmas-gift)**
