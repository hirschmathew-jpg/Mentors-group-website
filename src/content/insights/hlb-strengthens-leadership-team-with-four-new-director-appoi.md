### HLB Strengthens Leadership Team with Four New Director Appointments

](https://www.hlb.ie/graduate-programme-2024/)

The new directors are:

-   George Hardie has been appointed Corporate Finance Director, bringing extensive expertise in transaction advisory and strategic financial planning.
-   Louise Mollahan, who joins as Tax Director, with a strong background in advising businesses and individuals on complex tax matters.
-   Jack Sheehan, who joins the audit team, brings significant experience in assurance services and governance.
-   Lesley Whelan, as Head of Risk & Compliance, will focus on advisory and assisting clients with meeting their regulatory and risk requirements.

These appointments reflect HLB Ireland’s growth, underpinned by recent mergers and ongoing organic expansion. The firm is actively recruiting for additional roles across its service lines to meet increasing client demand and support its strategic plans.

Mark Butler, Managing Partner of HLB Ireland, commented: "_We are pleased to welcome George, Louise, Jack, and Lesley to their new roles. Each brings valuable expertise that enhances our ability to meet the evolving needs of our clients. As we continue to grow our team and expand our services, we remain focused on building strong partnerships and helping clients achieve their goals."_

Expanding Services to Meet Client Needs HLB Ireland has launched two new service lines in line with its growth strategy: Corporate Compliance and Digital Strategy for Business Owners. These offerings are designed to help clients navigate regulatory requirements efficiently and harness digital innovation to strengthen their businesses. At the same time, the firm continues to invest in its Audit, Tax, and Advisory teams, ensuring clients benefit from tailored solutions and expert guidance.
