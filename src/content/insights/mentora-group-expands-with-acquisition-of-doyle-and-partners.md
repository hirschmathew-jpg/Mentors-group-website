Mentora Group is pleased to announce the acquisition of Doyle & Partners, a Cork-based tax advisory practice with over twenty years of experience serving owner-managed businesses across Munster.

## A strategic fit

Doyle & Partners has built a reputation for delivering clear, practical tax advice to businesses navigating growth, succession and corporate restructuring. Their team of eight tax professionals will join HLB Ireland's advisory division, strengthening our presence across Cork, Kerry and Tipperary.

> "We've long admired how Doyle & Partners approach their work. Their commitment to long-term client relationships mirrors our own values exactly. This is a natural fit."
>
> — Mark Butler, Group Managing Partner

## What this means for clients

Existing Doyle & Partners clients will gain access to the full breadth of Mentora Group's services — including FutureRange's managed IT and cybersecurity offering, HLB Ireland's audit and corporate finance capability, and our growing wealth management practice.

The combined team will operate from Cork, with the Doyle & Partners office joining HLB Ireland's existing Cork presence to create a significantly stronger advisory team in the region.

## The eighth acquisition

This acquisition marks the eighth completed by HLB Ireland since beginning its acquisitions programme in 2017. Each acquisition has been selected on the basis of cultural alignment, complementary expertise, and shared commitment to the owner-managed business sector.

> "We are not acquiring for scale alone. Every firm that has joined us has brought something that made us better. Doyle & Partners continues that pattern."
>
> — Mark Butler

## About Mentora Group

Mentora Group is the holding company of HLB Ireland, an advisory and accounting firm, and FutureRange, an IT services firm. Formed in 2026, the group serves owner-managed businesses and private clients across Ireland.
