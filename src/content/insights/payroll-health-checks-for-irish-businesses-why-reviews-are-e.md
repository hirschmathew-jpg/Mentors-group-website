## Why Every Irish Business Needs a Payroll Health Check

For many SMEs, payroll is managed by a small [finance team](https://www.hlb.ie/services/corporate-finance-2/) or even a single administrator. While this can work day-to-day, problems often remain hidden until they cause serious issues.

### Payroll Complexity in Ireland

Payroll in Ireland is shaped by multiple layers of compliance:

-   PAYE Modernisation requires real-time reporting of wages, taxes, and deductions to Revenue.
-   Auto-enrolment pensions are set to begin rolling out, meaning employers must ensure systems are ready to handle mandatory pension contributions.
-   [Enhanced reporting requirements](https://www.revenue.ie/en/employing-people/becoming-an-employer-and-ongoing-obligations/err-jan-2024/index.aspx) introduced in 2024 add new obligations for tracking benefits, share options, and other non-cash perks.
-   Annual Budget changes frequently alter tax credits, PRSI thresholds, or USC bands.

With these moving parts, it’s easy for even well-run payroll systems to fall behind.

### Risks of Not Reviewing Payroll

Failing to review payroll regularly exposes businesses to:

-   Penalties from Revenue for under-reporting or incorrect submissions.
-   Dissatisfaction among staff if errors affect take-home pay, holiday pay, or benefits.
-   Missed opportunities to use automation and integrations that could save time.
-   Compliance breaches in areas such as pensions or data protection.

A payroll health check identifies these risks before they become problems.

## Frequently Asked Questions About Payroll Health Checks in Ireland

**What is a payroll health check?**

A payroll health check is a review of your company’s payroll processes to identify errors, compliance gaps, and opportunities for improvement.

**Why should Irish businesses get a payroll review?**

Because payroll rules are complex and constantly changing. A review ensures compliance with PAYE Modernisation, Revenue reporting, and upcoming auto-enrolment requirements.

**How often should payroll processes be reviewed?**

At least once a year, or sooner if new legislation or company changes affect payroll.

**What issues can a payroll health check uncover?**

It can highlight misclassified employees, incorrect PRSI categories, holiday pay errors, outdated software, and missed automation opportunities.

**Does a payroll review apply if payroll is outsourced?**

Yes. Even outsourced payroll providers can miss errors, so a second pair of expert eyes adds extra assurance.

**How will auto-enrolment affect Irish payroll?**

From 2024 onwards, employers must automatically enrol eligible employees into a pension scheme and manage contributions through payroll.

**What are the enhanced employer reporting requirements in Ireland?**

Since 2024, employers must report certain benefits and payments (such as small benefits, travel expenses, and share-based remuneration) to Revenue in real time.
